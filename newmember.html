Welcome! (please read!)

Thank you for your membership to Phorge Makerspace!

We look forward to helping you learn more about the tools and equipment that we have available in the makerspace. Since we began four years ago, we have seen an amazing community of makers come together to help make this happen and we are grateful to everyone who has helped contribute along the way.

There are a few things left to do in order for you to utilize the makerspace

  • Create an account at wyoma.org (our online learning platform)
  • Take the "General Makerspace Safety" course
  • Fill out the Accidental Waiver & Release of Liability Form
  • Join our Discord to chat live with other members

Once you've completed those tasks, go ahead and come on down to our open hours to meet the crew in person and complete the last few tasks needed for you to get started – this will include receiving a passcode for the door which will give you access to the makerspace 24 hours a day.

Remember, if you’re under 18 years old, please bring a parent or guardian with you.


If you have any other questions, we encourage you to ask them in our community Discord channel, or you can also find our traditional contact information below.

So thank you for joining and we're so happy to have you on board. Your support will help us expand our education platform and further our reach into the community of makers.
